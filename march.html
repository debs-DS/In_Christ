The March JESUS Challenge

J - Journal
E - Experience
S - Study
U - Understand
S - Speak

Today's Verse
Galatians 5:22-23
"But the Holy Spirit produces this kind of fruit in our lives: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control. There is no law against these things!"

previous days

JOURNAL

Spend about 15-20 minutes journaling to God. Tell Him about your day, how you are feeling, stuff like that. Confide in Him about anything and everything. You can start by saying "Hi Jesus" or "Dear Lord" - kind of like writing in a diary. You can also choose to write a letter, but just write something to Him. Thank Him and ask Him for anything you wish for Him to do in your life.

EXPERIENCE

Spend another 15-20 minutes sitting in His presence and experiencing His love. You can put on worship music and just sit there and let the Holy Spirit guide your thoughts.

STUDY

Take 5 minutes to write down the verse of the day & say it out loud. Also try to memorize it as it can be used as a weapon when the enemy tries to whisper lies into our ears or when we are unsure of what to do in certain situations.

UNDERSTAND

Take 10-15 minutes to understand the verse of the day. Pray before this portion & ask the Holy Spirit to help you understand anything He wants you to know.

Ask yourself these 3 Q's:

  • What does this verse teach you about God?
  • What does this verse teach you about yourself?
  • How can you apply this verse to your life?

SPEAK

Take 5 minutes to think about everything you've just learnt and experienced. Put it all into a prayer and tell God the changes you want Him to make in your life. Use your words to declare the verse of the day over your life. Use your words to speak goodness's truth over your life, so you can stand confidently in the authority you have in Jesus.

God Bless You!
