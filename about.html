about my blog

In this blog I will upload a bible verse along with some notes I wrote while studying it. This verse would occasionally be the daily bible verse.

The main purpose of this blog is to spread God's word.
But I also made it to encourage those who feel like God has left their side
or that He was never with them or those who seem to have relied on people
and now have nowhere to turn to.
I made this blog to tell them that they are not alone.
That there is a God and He never leaves His children behind.
Because he never left me. I just couldn't feel Him.
This blog is to help bring His children back and bring them closer to Him.
