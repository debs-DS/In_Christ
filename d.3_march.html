The March J.E.S.U.S. Challenge

Today's Bible Verse : Galatians 3:26-27

For you are all children through faith in Christ Jesus.
All all who have been united with Christ in baptism have put on new
clothes.

J - JOURNAL

Spend about 15 - 20 min journaling to God.
Tell him what's on your heart & talk to Him like you would a friend.
You could start it like you would a diary, "Dear Jesus" or "Dear Lord", anything really.
Thank him for anything and everything. Tell Him what bothers you, what you struggle with, areas in your life that you want Him to show His presence in.
Pour your heart out to Him.

E - EXPERIENCE

Spend another 15 - 20 mins sitting in His presence & experiencing His love. Put on some worship music & take some time to just listen & worship.

S - STUDY

Take 5 min to write down the verse of the day & say it out loud.
Memorize it and keep it in your heart.

U - UNDERSTAND

Take 10 - 15 min to understand the verse of the day. It's easy for the Bible to just feel like a bunch of words until we're actually intentional it & applying it to our lives. Pray before this and ask the Holy Spirit to help you understand anything He wants you to know.

Ask yourself these 3 Q'S:

  • What does this verse teach you about God?
  • What does this verse teach you about yourself?
  • How can you apply this verse to your life?

S - SPEAK

Take 5 mins to gather everything you've experienced up until this point & put it into prayer.
Use your words to declare the verse of the day over your life, add prayer requests & stand in agreement with God's truth.
Life and Death is in the power of your tongue. Use your words to speak God's truth over your life, so you can stand confidently in the authority you have in Jesus.
