The Beatitudes

  • Matthew 5:3
    - when you are lacking and you acknowledge this, turn to God
  • Matthew 5:4
    - if you are sad or depressed the Lord will comfort you
  • Matthew 5:5
    - the humble will be blessed and shall inherit all
  • Matthew 5:6
    - those who strive for justice will acquire it from the Lord
  • Matthew 5:7
    - the forgiving will be forgiven
  • Matthew 5:8
    - those of pure hearts will see God
  • Matthew 5:9
    - people who strive for peace will be called "The Children of God"
  • Matthew 5:10
    - the persecuted will obtain the kingdom of Heaven
